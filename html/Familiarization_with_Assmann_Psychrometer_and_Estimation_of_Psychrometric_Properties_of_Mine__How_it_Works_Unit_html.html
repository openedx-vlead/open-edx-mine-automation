How it works

Take the readings of Assmann Psychrometer and Aneroid Barometer.


                                                   

 Dry Bulb temperature, Wet Bulb temperature and barometric pressure from four different places.

                    

Then Calculate:

1.Vapour Pressure: Pw =Pws’- (tdb – twb) KPa

2. Specific Volume: V= (0.287T / (P - Pw))

3. Mixing Ratio: m= (622 * (Pw / (P - Pw)))

4. Relative Humidity: R.H=(e / ed) * 100%

5. Specific Humidity: SP=((0.622 * e ) / (P - 0.378 * e))

6. Mixing Ratio: m=0.622 * e/P.

7. Dew Point Temperature

8. Enthalpy: H= U+P * V
